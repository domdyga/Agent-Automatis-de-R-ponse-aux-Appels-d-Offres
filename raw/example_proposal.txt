ACME CONSULTING — PAST PROPOSAL EXTRACT
=======================================

Project: Enterprise Data Platform for Ministry of Finance
Client:  French Ministry of Finance
Year:    2023

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
Acme Consulting proposed a comprehensive, cloud-native data platform designed to centralise,
process, and analyse financial data across 12 ministerial departments. Our team of 18 experts
delivered the project within 9 months, under budget by 8%.

TECHNICAL APPROACH
------------------
We adopted a modern data lakehouse architecture using Apache Spark for distributed processing,
Delta Lake for ACID-compliant storage, and dbt for transformation pipelines.
Security was enforced via role-based access control (RBAC) and end-to-end encryption at rest
and in transit.

METHODOLOGY
-----------
Phase 1 — Discovery & Architecture (weeks 1–4):
  Stakeholder interviews, as-is analysis, technical requirements gathering.

Phase 2 — Platform Build (weeks 5–24):
  Infrastructure provisioning (Terraform), CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions),
  ingestion connectors for 7 legacy systems.

Phase 3 — Migration & Testing (weeks 25–32):
  Data migration, UAT with department leads, performance benchmarking.

Phase 4 — Handover & Training (weeks 33–36):
  Knowledge transfer sessions, runbook documentation, hypercare support.
